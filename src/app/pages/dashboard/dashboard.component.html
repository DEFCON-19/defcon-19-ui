KNOW HOW IT SPREADS
There is currently No Vaccine to prevent COVID-19

After seeing many real-time dashboards presenting COVID-19 statistics all over the news, we were still left with some unanswered questions. How do the measures we are taking impact disease spread and fatality? What can we do to protect ourselves and our communities? Are we doing enough? What can we learn from this situation to be better prepared in the future?

TAKE STEPS TO PROTECT YOURSELF

To answer these questions, we gathered and analyzed data from 201 countries around the world. Looking at demographics, health indicators, infrastructure and protective measures, such as face masks and social distancing, we determined the relationship that each of these factors has with infection rates and deaths. Here, we present a visualization tool based on our calculations. You can adjust each of the parameters to see how they affect the outcomes of COVID-19 for Norway.

AVOID CLOSE CONTACT

Our goal for Hack the Crisis Norway is to launch a proof of concept, an informational and educational solution whose main purpose is to save lives. We want users to experience the effect that their choices have on their own and their communities’ health. By playing with the parameters of the model to simulate different scenarios for the COVID-19 outbreak, you can see, for example, exactly how important social distancing is for limiting disease spread. Knowledge is power, and our actions have a big impact in how the crisis plays out! #HacktheCrisisNorway

While we are happy with our prototype, we have many plans for the future! In the coming weeks and months, our objective is to facilitate the spread of factual information about the pandemic, empower users to proactively embrace infection control measures, and provide governments with an engaging public health education tool. Moreover, technology has reached a point for simulation of scenarios to be viable. As a highly compatible and skilled team that has developed a working prototype (back-end and front-end), we believe we are ideally positioned to become the most reputable player within crisis management software, both in terms of development and consulting.
